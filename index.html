FAQ

Do you install at my location?

Yes. Tint Happens is a mobile service for Bradford and the GTA. Exact availability depends on weather, space, and schedule.

Do you cut film inside the van?

No. Window patterns are prepared beforehand on a professional plotter, then installed at your location. No fake sci-fi van laboratory nonsense.

What does VLT mean?

VLT means Visible Light Transmission, the amount of light passing through the glass. Lower percentage means darker tint.

How do I get the final price?

Use the quote builder and text the setup. Final price is confirmed after vehicle details and install conditions.
